📱 LUMINA: University Announcements and Events Awareness App
USER MANUAL
1. Introduction
Overview of Lumina

Lumina is a university announcements and events awareness app that allows users to:

View campus updates
Interact with posts
Receive notifications

Access and available screens vary by role (Student, Staff, Admin).

Purpose of the Application
Keep students informed about announcements and events
Help staff publish announcements/events and monitor engagement
Help admins manage users and academic structures (campus/college/course) and view analytics
Key Functionality
Announcement & event feed with search, filtering, and sorting
Post details with images, likes, comments, and replies
Event RSVP (“Going”), participant counts, and participant list
In-app notifications + push notification support (configurable)
Role-based dashboards (Admin/Staff) with statistics and charts
Admin management modules (students, staff, campus, college, course, activity logs)
2. System Requirements
📱 Android smartphone: Android 7.0 (Nougat) or newer
🌐 Internet connection: Required for login, loading posts/events, and syncing notifications
🔐 Others:
A valid account
Notifications enabled (recommended)
3. Getting Started
3.1 Installation
Download the APK from your school/organization
Open the APK on your phone
Allow “Install unknown apps” if prompted
Complete installation
Launch LUMINA
3.2 Account Registration
Tap Create Account from the login screen
Required details:
First name
Last name
Email (@ms.bulsu.edu.ph)
Campus
College
Course

📧 After registration, a generated password is sent to your email (if configured).

3.3 Login
Enter email/username and password
Tap Login
Redirected based on role (Admin / Staff / Student)

8. FAQs

Q: Can I log in using a username?
✔ Yes

Q: Can I use a personal email?
❌ No, only BulSU emails

Q: Can’t log in after registering?
👉 Account may need approval

Q: How to join an event?
👉 Tap Going

Q: Why can’t I join?
👉 Event may be full

Q: How to disable notifications?
👉 Turn off in Configurations or Android settings
